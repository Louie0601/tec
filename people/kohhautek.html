Koh Hau-Tek

Singapore


Deputy Chief Medical Officer of Jiahui Health

1. Deputy Chief Medical Officer co-overseeing clinical services and actively involved in clinical operations and product development at Jiahui Health;
2. Chair of the Jiahui vaccination and clinical products committees;
3. Sits on various committees including privileging & credentialing, clinical standards, medical executive council, medical policies, peer review;
4. Managed another medical group prior to Jiahui, responsible for all operations of the group’s healthcare assets, across 6 cities in China;
5. Practicing physician for over 20 years in other major medical groups in Singapore and the Republic of Ireland;
6. Have sat on and presided over Medical Boards and Clinical Governance committees in several medical groups, including public-listed Parkway Health (IHH) and Healthway Medical Group in Singapore;
7. Vice-President, International Business Development, with focus on strategy and business development at Certis CISCO Pte Ltd, driving merger and acquisition activities;
8. 1st class Honors medical degree & an intercalated Bachelor of Medical Science at National University of Ireland;
9. Membership to the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland;
10. Master in Medicine (Surgery), Singapore;
11. Registered Family Physician with the Singapore Medical Council and a registered Designated Workplace Doctor with Singapore’s Ministry of Manpower;
12. Completed an Advanced Management Program at Nanyang-UC Berkeley;
13. Master Degree of Business Administration (MBA) from Nanyang Business School in Nanyang Technological University Singapore;
14. Adjunct lecturer in Occupational Medicine at the National University of Singapore



Dr. Koh has been working closely with TEC as a partner in having COVID-19 vaccinations for expats and TEC members. In this time of COVID in China, Jiahui International Hospital continues to provide a critical support pillar by having COVID-19 vaccinations in a credible world-class facility with strong operational capabilities, and a good track record and well-trained medical/nursing staff.

In addition, Jiahui International Hospital and Jiahui Clinics have been open throughout the Shanghai lockdown recently, continuing to provide much needed healthcare services in the clinics, outpatients, emergency department, inpatients, and surgeries in multiple medical departments.

Dr. Koh continues to support TEC through provision of cost-effective world-class healthcare and its related clinical capabilities.
